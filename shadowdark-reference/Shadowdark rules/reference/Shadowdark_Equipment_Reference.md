### Weapon Properties

**Finesse**: Use DEX instead of STR for attack and damage
**Two-handed**: Requires both hands to wield
**Versatile**: Can be used one or two-handed (damage shown as 1H/2H)
**Thrown**: Can be thrown for ranged attack
**Ranged**: Designed for ranged combat
**Reach**: Can attack targets at close range from near
**Loading**: Takes action to reload

### Weapon Selection Guide

**Best Overall**:
- Longsword (1d8, versatile, can use with shield)
- Longbow (1d8, excellent range)
- Greatsword (1d12, maximum damage)

**Best for Thieves**:
- Shortsword (1d6 finesse, DEX-based)
- Dagger (1d4 finesse, concealable)

**Best Budget**:
- Spear (1d6, only 5 sp, versatile)
- Club (1d4, basically free)

### Armor Selection Guide

**Fighters/Priests** (all armor):
- Early: Chainmail + Shield (AC 15+DEX)
- Late: Plate + Shield (AC 17)

**Thieves** (leather only):
- Leather armor (AC 11+DEX)
- High DEX is crucial

**Wizards** (no armor):
- AC = 10 + DEX
- Stay far from enemies

## Gear Slots

### Carrying Capacity
**Total Slots = 10 + STR modifier**

| STR Mod | Slots |
|---------|-------|
| -2 | 8 |
| -1 | 9 |
| +0 | 10 |
| +1 | 11 |
| +2 | 12 |
| +3 | 13 |
| +4 | 14 |

### What Takes Slots
- Most items: 1 slot each
- Armor worn: 0 slots
- Weapons wielded: 0 slots
- First backpack: Free
- First coin pouch: Free
- 100 coins: 1 slot
- 20 arrows/bolts: 1 slot
- 10 iron spikes: 1 slot
- 3 rations: 1 slot
- 10 gems: 1 slot

### Over-Encumbered
If carrying more than your limit:
- Disadvantage on all physical checks
- Move at half speed
- Can't run

## Shopping Tips

### Must-Haves for Every Character
1. Light source (torches minimum)
2. Rations (need for resting)
3. Rope (endless utility)
4. Flint & steel (light those torches)

### High-Value Purchases
- **Extra torches**: Light is life
- **Oil flasks**: Lanterns last longer, can be used as weapons
- **Iron spikes**: Spike doors, create anchors, test for traps
- **Mirror**: See around corners safely
- **Pole**: Poke suspicious things from a distance

### Money Management
- Keep some gold in reserve for emergencies
- Don't spend everything on gear - save for carousing XP
- Mithral armor is expensive but worth it for stealth characters

## Hirelings and Retainers

### Hiring Help
- **Torchbearer**: 1 sp/day - Carries light, basic tasks
- **Porter**: 2 sp/day - Carries gear, won't fight
- **Guard**: 1 gp/day - Will fight, basic equipment
- **Specialist**: 5+ gp/day - Skills like lockpicking, healing

### Hireling Morale
Hirelings check morale in dangerous situations:
- DC 12 WIS to stay in scary situations
- DC 15 WIS if combat occurs
- Bonus if treated well, penalty if mistreated

### Retainer Rules
- Share in treasure (half or quarter share typical)
- May level up over time
- Can become full party members
- Loyalty builds through shared danger
