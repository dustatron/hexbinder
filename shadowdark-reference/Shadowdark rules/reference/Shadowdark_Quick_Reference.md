# Shadowdark RPG - Master Quick Reference

## Core Mechanic
**d20 + modifier vs DC** ‚Äî Roll equal or higher to succeed

### Difficulty Classes
| Easy | Normal | Hard | Extreme |
|------|--------|------|---------|
| DC 9 | DC 12 | DC 15 | DC 18 |

### Advantage/Disadvantage
- **Advantage**: Roll 2d20, keep highest
- **Disadvantage**: Roll 2d20, keep lowest

---

## Combat Quick Reference

### Initiative
**d20 + DEX each round** ‚Äî Highest goes first, then clockwise

### Attacks
| Type | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Melee | d20 + STR + weapon vs AC |
| Ranged | d20 + DEX + weapon vs AC |
| Damage | Weapon die + STR (melee) or DEX (ranged) |

### Special Results
- **Natural 20**: Double damage dice (CRITICAL)
- **Natural 1**: GM discretion (FUMBLE)

### Combat Conditions
| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| Surprised | Attackers have advantage |
| Partial cover | Attacker has disadvantage |
| Full cover | Can't be targeted |
| Rough terrain | Half movement |

### Morale
**DC 15 WIS** when at half HP or half numbers ‚Äî Fail = flee

## Death & Dying

| Event | Rule |
|-------|------|
| 0 HP | Roll d4 + CON = rounds until death |
| Natural 20 while dying | Rise with 1 HP |
| Stabilize | DC 15 INT check by ally |
| Knockout | Choose to KO instead of kill |

## Spellcasting Quick Reference

### Spell Check
**d20 + casting stat vs DC (10 + Spell Tier)**
- Wizards: INT
- Priests: WIS

### Results
| Roll | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Success | Spell works |
| Failure | Spell lost until rest |
| Natural 20 | Double one numerical effect |
| Natural 1 (Wizard) | ALL spells lost + Mishap |
| Natural 1 (Priest) | THAT spell lost until penance + rest |

### Focus Spells
- Success: Continues until next turn
- Failure: Ends (spell not lost)
- Taking damage: Immediate check to maintain

### Scrolls & Wands
- DC 10 + Spell Tier
- Scrolls: One use, failure destroys
- Wands: Nat 1 = breaks permanently

## Resting

### Requirements
- 1 ration + 8 hours sleep + safe location

### Benefits
- Regain ALL HP
- Regain all spells/abilities
- Recover stat damage

### Interrupted
- DC 12 CON or no benefit (still lose ration)

## Key Design Principles

1. **Roll only when it matters** ‚Äî consequences, skill, time pressure
2. **Player skill over character builds** ‚Äî clever play wins
3. **Light is life** ‚Äî real-time torch tracking
4. **XP for treasure** ‚Äî not for killing
5. **Rulings over rules** ‚Äî GM adjudicates
6. **Death is real** ‚Äî consequences matter
