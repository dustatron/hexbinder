# Obojima Bestiary - Shadowdark Conversion

## Conversion Notes

**Formula:**
- LV = 5e CR (round appropriately, bosses CR 17+ scaled to LV 10-15)
- HP = ~40% of 5e (or LV × d8 + CON)
- AC = Same as 5e or -1
- Attack = +LV (rarely exceeds LV)
- Damage = Simplified (typically 1d8 per attack for standard enemies)

**Stat Scaling by Level:**
- LV 0: HP 1-10, AC 9-12, +0, 1d4
- LV 1: HP 5-15, AC 11-14, +1, 1d6-1d8
- LV 2: HP 10-20, AC 12-14, +2, 1d6-1d8
- LV 3: HP 15-30, AC 13-15, +3, 1d8-1d10
- LV 4-5: HP 25-45, AC 14-16, +4-5, 1d8-2d6
- LV 6-8: HP 40-70, AC 15-17, +6-8, 2d6-2d10
- LV 10+: HP 70+, AC 16+, +10+, 3d8+
