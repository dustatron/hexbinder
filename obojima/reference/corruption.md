# THE CORRUPTION
*In-game reference*

## QUICK FACTS
| | |
|-|-|
| **Look** | Black tar, iridescent purple/green/red veins |
| **Origin** | From sea. Earthquake + tsunami. 3 months ago. |
| **Spread** | Eastern coast, Brackwater. Slowing. Seeping underground. |

---

## INFECTION

**SAFE:**
- Brief touch
- Breathing in corrupted zone
- Touching corrupted creature/tree/object

**DANGEROUS:**
- Direct contact for several minutes
- Open wounds exposed to corruption
- Ingesting corrupted water/fish

**PROTECTION:**
- Special armor, potions, spells eliminate risk
- **Stonefish venom** slows corruption progression (addictive)

---

## CORRUPTION EFFECTS (d6)
*Roll when PC/NPC becomes infected. From Corrupted Ranger subclass.*

| d6 | Ailment | Curse | Power |
|----|---------|-------|-------|
| 1 | **Graying Vision** | World loses color. Disadvantage on sight Perception. | Gain 15ft Blindsight. Auto-detect visual illusions. |
| 2 | **Failing Lungs** | Ache. Gasp for air. Disadvantage CON saves. | Can steal breath (target prone, no reactions). |
| 3 | **Losing Feeling** | Hands/arms burn with cold flame only you see. | Ethereal flames boost necrotic damage. |
| 4 | **Blackened Veins** | Corruption stains skin, seeps from pores. Visible. | +2 Intimidation. Corruption drawn to you. |
| 5 | **Vertigo** | World spins suddenly. Disadvantage DEX checks. | Sense corruption nearby. |
| 6 | **Wasting** | Must eat twice as much or gain exhaustion. | Can safely consume corrupted food/water. |

**PROGRESSION:**
- Corruption stains skin, seeps from pores
- Slowly replaces flesh with unearthly power
- Untreated: death in days OR transformation
- **Stonefish venom**: Slows progression. Highly addictive. Source: Venomous Rex.

**CORRUPTED RANGER ORIGINS (for PCs):**
- First responder when corruption hit shores (only survivor?)
- Explorer who stumbled into corruption pocket
- Cursed by an evil witch

**CURSE MARKERS (Corrupted Ranger mechanic):**
- Gain 1 when damaged (2 on crit)
- Consume on hit: +1d4 necrotic per marker
- Bonus action: gain max markers
- Max: 2 (lvl 3) → 3 (lvl 5) → 4 (lvl 11) → 5 (lvl 17)
