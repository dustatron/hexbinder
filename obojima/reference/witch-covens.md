## Joining a Coven

**Process:**
1. Young mages typically apprentice with a coven Witch first
2. Talented solo Witches may be recruited directly
3. Coven ultimately decides whether to extend invitation

**The Oath:**
- Special ritual binding Witch to coven (and coven to Witch)
- Grants access to coven's power, magical traditions, and protection
- Some covens offer lodging; many Witches keep own dwellings

**Oathbreaker Curse:**
- Breaking coven oath triggers a curse
- Manifests physically to mark the oathbreaker
- Nature and severity varies by coven

## The Covens

### Fish Head Coven
*Location: The Witching Tower, Gale Fields*

The most powerful and influential coven on Obojima. Controls discovery and trade of magical ingredients across the island.

**Leadership:** Council of Three and Thirty
- **Yolikanter Crogiss** - Paranoid nakudama, warding magic specialist
- **Bim of the Beasts** - Enigmatic human, speaks only through animals
- **Brass Eyes** - Withered human with brass-orbed eyes, exists in both Physical and Spirit Realms
- **The Thirty** - Amalgamated spirit entity

**Notable Features:**
- Dozens of affiliated Witches island-wide
- Possesses TWO Almanac volumes (Gale Fields + Shallows)
- The Sanctum - hidden library with chot-tos (animated statues holding spells/knowledge)
- Closed Fists vaults - ingredient stores + dangerous magic items
- Magical hedgerow alerts leaders when crossed

**Dynamics:** Smaller covens envy or bemoan their power. Inter-coven hostilities usually sportsmanlike rivalry, but feuds and arcane duels occur.

## Ingredient Testing

**Arcane Detection Kits (ADKs):**
- Used to determine if substance has potion ingredient properties
- During downtime: 1 hour to test up to 6 substances
- GM reveals which are ingredients and their attributes

**Alternative:** Witches usually willing to identify ingredients for coin or trade.
