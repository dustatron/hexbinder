# Obojima Geographic Reference

> Source: *Obojima: Tales from the Tall Grass* sourcebook
> Purpose: Hex map placement guide for hexbinder

## Island Overview

- **~300 miles** west-to-east
- **Mount Arbora** (14,000 ft) central peak, visible from everywhere
- **Design rule:** "The further east you go, the more dangerous"
- Ringed by reef system called **The Shallows**
- Rivers flow outward from Mount Arbora (meltwater-fed)

## Rivers

- All rivers flow OUTWARD from Mount Arbora (meltwater-fed)
- A major river flows south from Mt Arbora, crosses the E-W road at Okiri
- Din River flows to Opal Falls â†’ Blue Back Lake (Coastal Highlands)
- 4 great river spirits, each named after their river
- Gift of Shuritashi has "gentle rivers meandering toward coast"

## Roads

- **East-West Road:** Major route crossing Gale Fields, maintained by Courier Brigade
- Connects western settlements through Fort Harglo / Witching Tower to eastern regions
- **Pelican Way:** Dirt road in Brackwater Wetlands (partially destroyed by tsunami)
- **Courier Brigade** HQ in Yatamon, outposts across island
