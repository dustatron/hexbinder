Everyone on Obojima contacts spirits to some degree, but your interactions have always occurred with peculiar regularity. Special kinship with spirits that few share. Love their food, culture, and people more than average material being. Draws fascination, suspicion, and confusion—but locals enlist your talents when spirit stuff gets weird.
