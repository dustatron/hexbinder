# Obojima Magic Items

48 magic items unique to Obojima. Most magic items on the island are used by everyday islanders—on farms, in bakeries, bookshops, etc.

---

## Common Items

### Cloud-Touched Boomerang
*Weapon (Boomerang), Attunement*

On hit, activate swirling vortex: DC 12 STR save or Prone.

---

### Coin's Edge
*Wondrous Item*

Flip coin into air → permanently transforms into mundane Dagger.

---

### Hover Hopper
*Wondrous Item, Attunement*

Magic balloons. While inflated: fall damage halved, use DEX for jump distance.

---

### Painter's Sun Hat
*Wondrous Item*

Magic action + command word: hat floats, projects illusory image of familiar Humanoid (always wearing the hat). DC 10 Investigation to see through.

---

### Scuttling Lantern
*Wondrous Item*

Magic action to light (30ft bright/30ft dim). Command to move 20ft along surfaces or through water. Follows you within 10ft. Deactivates if >500ft away.

---

### Sticky Hand
*Wondrous Item*

Children's toy. Cast Retrieve cantrip while holding.

---

*Wondrous Item*
