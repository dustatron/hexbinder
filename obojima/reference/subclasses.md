# Obojima Subclasses

Eleven subclasses intertwined with the island of Obojima, its culture, and its creatures.

---

## Belly Brewer (Barbarian)

Masters of brewing potions on the fly—directly in their guts. While others must painstakingly prepare potions with finesse, Belly Brewers channel their inner hunger to unlock the latent magic within raw ingredients without any preparation.

### Features

**Level 3: Belly Concoction**
When you enter Rage, choose one concoction type:
- **Combat** - Rubber body deflects ranged attacks (1d12+CON+level reduction). Stretch arms for +5ft reach and +1d6 damage.
- **Utility** - Swallow any mouth-sized object. If non-magical, expend Hit Die to heal (roll+CON).
- **Whimsy** - Random effects each turn: teleport 10ft, take 1d4 necrotic for ability check bonus, or Help as Bonus Action.

**Level 3: Side Effects**
Know Prestidigitation and Thaumaturgy cantrips (CON casting).

**Level 6: Quick Brew**
Choose a common potion. Activate as Bonus Action 1/Long Rest. DC = 8+CON+Prof.

**Level 10: Lingering Effects**
After Long Rest, choose condition to gain Advantage on saves against.

**Level 14: Mighty Quick Brew**
Learn additional common and uncommon potion for Quick Brew.

## College of Masks (Bard)

Theater actors capable of losing themselves in a role using masks imbued with magic. Through these masks, Bards can mimic others and take on attributes of the personas their masks depict.

### Features

**Level 3: Tools of Performance**
Craft 2 masks (additional at levels 7, 11, 15). Don/swap as Utilize or Bonus Action.

**Level 3: Copycat**
Magic action, expend Bardic Inspiration to cast Alter Self (Change Appearance) without concentration. Gain 5 temp HP and equipment.

**Level 6: Many-Masked Performance**
1 minute performance. Targets (up to Bard level) within 60ft must save or be Charmed, Frightened, or Unconscious for 10 min. 1/Long Rest.

**Level 14: Gift of Theater**
Magic action to give mask to ally within 5ft for 1 minute. 1/Short or Long Rest.

### Masks
- **Demon** (5+): Arcane Memory, Demon Eye (see through objects 15ft)
- **Fish**: Swim Speed, water breathing, Water Bullet/Whelm Weapon prepared
- **Fox**: Move through enemy space, +1 AC, Reaction for Advantage on DEX saves
- **Golem** (5+): +1d8 damage, ignore Heavy Disadvantage, double damage to objects
- **Harpy** (11+): Fly Speed, Resistance to Psychic
- **Old Man**: Detect Magic/Identify without slots
- **Protagonist**: Temp HP on Initiative, mark antagonist for Advantage, reduce attack rolls
- **Sea Urchin**: Heal with Bardic Inspiration, Restrain creatures
- **Spirit** (7+): Speak with Dead, move through Spirit Realm
- **Stone**: Athletics proficiency, reduce B/P/S by 2, Advantage vs push/pull/Prone

## Circle of the Petal (Druid)

Druids attuned to the delicate dance of petals upon the breeze. They draw power from the spirit of Obojima, the steady island breeze, and the ephemeral beauty and resilience of flowers.

### Features

**Level 3: Petal Dance**
Bonus Action, expend Wild Shape for petal cloud (1 hour). +1 AC. Bonus Action ranged attack 1d6 Slashing (scales at 5/11/17). Reaction to reduce damage by Druid level + WIS (dissipates cloud).

**Level 6: Petal Beast Mimicry**
Magic action to summon Wild Spirit (uses stat block). 2 uses (3 at 11, 4 at 16). 1/Long Rest. At 11+, spend 2 uses for 2 spirits.

**Level 10: Spirit of Obojima**
Advantage on Survival to find paths. Create petal wall (60ft long, 15ft high). Nothing passes except designated creatures. 1/Long Rest.

**Level 14: Winds of Revival**
When reduced to 0 HP, regain half HP max instead. Reaction for 10ft emanation: STR save or 2d8 Slashing + pushed 15ft. 1/Long Rest.

## Spirit-Fused (Fighter)

Vessel of a dying spirit. The spirit's essence coexists within the Fighter, granting abilities to channel latent energy into objects for magical effects.

### Features

**Level 3: Arcane Quirk**
Choose visual/auditory quirks that manifest when using subclass features.

**Level 3: Channel Essence**
4 Essence Dice (d6). Add to attack or damage rolls. Regain on Short/Long Rest. More dice at 7/10/15/18.

**Level 3: Object Channeling**
Learn 1 Channeling Option. Use 1/Short or Long Rest.

**Level 7: Spirit Battery**
Gain Spirit type. Learn Jolt cantrip (CON). Power machines 1 hour. Replace weapon damage with Jolt damage once/turn.

**Level 7: Improved Object Channeling**
Learn another option. Uses = 1+CON mod.

**Level 10: Improvised Armor**
Reaction to add Essence Die to AC against attack.

**Level 15: Backup Battery**
5 Second Wind uses. Auto-trigger when HP drops below Fighter level.

**Level 18: Junk Master**
Learn another option. Regain to 2 uses on Initiative.

### Channeling Options
- **Ballooning Bag**: Float 30ft up, or Feather Fall reaction
- **Entangling Cord**: Pull target 15ft
- **Find the Thing**: Locate Object 1 mile, 1 hour, no concentration
- **First Age Steed**: Summon vehicle (60ft Speed, 1 hour)
- **Goggled Vision**: Darkvision 60ft, Advantage vs illusions
- **Leaping Sneakers**: Jump spell 1 hour
- **Pocket Voice**: Bind voice to object 1 hour
- **Water Jettison**: Disengage + pushed 10ft

## Sheep Dragon Shepherd (Monk)

Those who live and train among wandering sheep dragons learn to care deeply for those around them. Requires resilience, compassion, and a kind heart.

### Features

**Level 3: Herding Sheep**
Shepherd Crooks have Light property. Bonus Action spend Focus to move willing creature (15ft toward you, 10ft other). More targets at 6/11/17.

**Level 6: Wind Shot**
Ranged Unarmed Strike (60ft). Spend 1 Focus for STR save or pushed 10ft/Prone.

**Level 6: Intercepting Maneuver**
Reaction + 1 Focus when ally within 10ft hit. Reduce damage by Martial Arts die + DEX.

**Level 11: Take to the Skies**
Step of the Wind grants Fly Speed. Disengage + Dash without spending Focus.

**Level 17: Guide to the Herd**
When using Patient Defense for both Disengage/Dodge, spend 1 extra Focus for allies within 30ft to gain same benefits.

## Oath of the River (Paladin)

Guidance from shifting waters and great creatures within. Model life after tranquil pools and rushing rapids—flow around obstacles when possible, crash through when necessary.

### Tenets
- Forgiveness can be as sharp as any blade
- Judgment is reserved for those truly deserving
- If I must battle, I fight with all passion and strength
- I will not abandon my duty when others are counting on me

### Features

**Level 3: Blessed Pool**
Bonus Action, heal 2d6 HP to up to 2×CHA mod creatures within 30ft. 1/Short or Long Rest.

**Level 3: Rushing Rapids**
Channel Divinity: 20ft emanation, Large or smaller creatures pushed 5ft outside.

**Level 7: Aura of the River**
Aura of Protection is Difficult Terrain for enemies. First melee hit/turn moves Large or smaller target 10ft.

**Level 15: Shielding Spirit**
Reaction to halve DEX save damage. Expend slot to extend to allies within 10ft equal to slot level.

**Level 20: Form of the River**
Bonus Action for 1 min: +15ft Speed, 60ft Swim, Advantage on saves, 2d8 damage when moving creatures. 1/Long Rest or 5th level slot.

## Corrupted (Ranger)

Afflicted by the mysterious Corruption illness. This foul magic stains coastlines black and now resides within your body, both cursing and empowering you.

### Features

**Level 3: First Manifestation**
Taking damage grants curse markers (2 on crit). Max 2 (3 at 5, 4 at 11, 5 at 17). On hit, consume all markers for +1d4 Necrotic each. Bonus Action to gain max markers.

**Level 3: Second Manifestation**
Replace STR/DEX check with 10+Ranger level. 2/Long Rest.

**Level 7: Third Manifestation**
Expend marker for +1d4 to attack roll. Reaction to reduce damage by Ranger level. 1/Short or Long Rest.

**Level 11: Fourth Manifestation**
Choose ailment:
- **Failing Lungs**: Magic action to steal breath (Prone, no Reactions, consume markers for damage). Or give breath for DEX Advantage + Speed.
- **Graying Vision**: Blindsight 15ft, auto-detect easy illusions.
- **Losing Feeling**: Bonus Action to curse target, markers deal 1d6 instead of 1d4. 1/Short or Long Rest.

**Level 15: Fifth Manifestation**
Bonus Action: double Speed, +2 AC, 2 extra attacks, max markers on all attacks. Second use causes 1 Exhaustion + drop to 1 HP. 1/Long Rest after second use.

## Waxwork (Rogue)

Array of tools and tricks using wax, smarts, and magic. Abilities stem from arcane heritage tied to witchcraft—craft magical candles with diverse effects.

### Features

**Level 3: Conjure Flame**
Know Dancing Lights (as candles) and Produce Flame. INT or CHA casting.

**Level 3: Wax Enchantments**
Learn 4 enchantments (2 more at 9/13/17). 3+ Wick Points (increases with Rogue level).

**Level 9: Burn Cycle**
Spend Wick Points instead of Sneak Attack dice for Cunning Strike. Can change damage to Fire.

**Level 13: Advanced Enchantments**
Enhanced effects for select enchantments.

**Level 17: Spirit Flame**
Bonus Action, pink candle burns 1 min: reduce Wick costs by 1, Fire Immunity, Spirits have Disadvantage vs you. 1/Long Rest.

### Key Enchantments
- **Blooming Light** (1 WP): Dim light hides from Divination
- **Candle Pop** (2 WP): DEX save or Restrained
- **Delicacy** (1 WP): Healing candle (1d6 HP)
- **Detonate** (3+ WP): 20ft sphere, 2d6 Fire + 2d8 Thunder
- **Iron Seal** (1 WP): Magically seal door/container
- **Waxlings** (2 WP): Find Familiar as candle creature
- **Wick Whip** (3 WP): 10ft reach whip, 3d4 Fire, 1 min

## Oni Kin (Sorcerer)

Blood bears ancient connection to oni. Harness lineage magic while gradually transforming into embodiment of oni's fearsome might.

### Features

**Level 3: Oni Spells**
Additional prepared spells at Sorcerer levels.

**Level 3: Arcane Prison**
Oni traits manifest as you spend Sorcery Points:
- **Eyes**: CHA Advantage vs one creature, but it may become Hostile
- **Horns**: Melee cantrip attacks +1d6 Piercing, cantrip opportunity attacks
- **Skin**: Heal spell level + CON when casting (not with Tongue)
- **Tongue**: Single-target spell Bonus Action: WIS save or Prone/flee
- **Hair**: +2 AC

**Level 6: Ogre Manipulation**
Bonus Action to activate trait early (lasts 1 min). 1/Long Rest.

**Level 14: Boiling Power**
Enhanced traits: Eyes gets Zone of Truth, Horns +1d12, Skin heals 2× spell level, Tongue causes -5 AC and Perception.

**Level 18: Transformation**
Magic action to manifest all traits for 1 min. Resistance to B/P/S, Immunity to Charmed/Frightened. 1/Long Rest or 5 SP.

## Lantern Patron (Warlock)

Pact with powerful spirit, channeled through special lanterns that burn with patron's essence. The illumination grants an array of abilities.

### Features

**Level 3: Lantern Spells**
Additional prepared spells.

**Level 3: Illuminating Aura**
Lantern as focus. Bonus Action for 1 min: 20ft bright/20ft dim. Reaction to add CHA to ally saves in bright light. Enemies in dim light take half Warlock level Radiant on failed saves. 1/Short or Long Rest.

**Level 3: Revealing Light**
Magic action for maroon light (10ft bright/10ft dim). Invisible visible in bright light. 1 hour. 1/Short or Long Rest.

**Level 6: Dangerous Tool**
Aura continues if you drop lantern. Control as Spiritual Weapon (no concentration) for 1 min. 1/Long Rest or spell slot.

**Level 6: Oracle Illumination**
Magic action to see/hear through light source you know for 10 min. 1/Long Rest per source.

**Level 10: Brilliant Illumination**
Magic action during aura: CON save or 2d8+level Radiant + Blinded. 1/Short or Long Rest.

**Level 14: Brighter Still**
Aura at will. Magic action for Find the Path effect. 1/Long Rest.

## Origami Mage (Wizard)

Unique magic combining precision, creativity, and imagination. Transform paper into awe-inspiring manifestations of elemental forces, mystical beings, and creatures.

### Features

**Level 3: An Arcane Art**
Bonus Action to create Origami Construct bound to you or creature within 60ft. Lasts half Wizard level hours. AC = spell save DC, Immune to Poison/Psychic. Uses = INT mod/Long Rest.

**Level 3: Origami Familiar**
Find Familiar always prepared. Appears as origami. Construct type. Gains:
- **Camouflage**: Flatten to paper form
- **Last Resort**: Dive to Blind creature (1 min, STR check to remove)
- **Reconnaissance**: Record sounds for 1 min, return as transcript

**Level 6: Binding Release**
Bonus Action to move Construct to creature within 30ft.

**Level 6: Paper Path**
Touch spells through familiar don't require Reaction.

**Level 10: Origami Servant**
Unseen Servant always prepared, no slot, visible origami. Duration = 1+INT hours. See Invisible. Control all servants with one Bonus Action.

**Level 14: Arcane Refresh**
When Construct destroyed, regain 10 HP or spell slot (2nd or lower). Reaction to negate Construct damage 1/Long Rest.

### Origami Constructs
- **Bird**: Perception proficiency, +2 Slashing on ranged attacks
- **Cat**: Stealth proficiency, 1d6 Slashing melee follow-up (scales)
- **Crab**: +1 AC, Athletics proficiency
- **Dragon**: Disadvantage on Stealth, no Invisible, 1d4 Fire/turn. Explodes for 2d6 Fire in 5ft
- **Frog**: Acrobatics proficiency, Reaction to add INT to DEX save (destroys frog)
