# ANCESTRIES OF OBOJIMA
[[Obojima Tales From The Tall Grass 2024.pdf#page=158|pg 158]]

The core ancestries of Obojima are **humans**, **elves**, **nakudama**, and **dara**. Other D&D ancestries can be adapted (see source pg 169).

---

## HUMANS
[[Obojima Tales From The Tall Grass 2024.pdf#page=158|pg 158]] | *Majority population, connected to First Age*

**Overview:** Most populous ancestry on Obojima. Prefer modest farming/fishing lives but carry the brightest spark of adventure. Many explore the island's mysteries before settling down.

**Origin:** Arrived by boat, ships scuttled on landing. Nakudama records call it "the return of the humans" - suggesting connection to First Age. AHA historians note all First Age depictions show only humans.

**Traits:**
- First to inhabit First Age buildings
- Spread quickly, founded Yatamon, Bidlow, other major towns
- Strong curiosity about First Age mysteries
- Can develop spirit connections through rituals, may become shamans/sages
- Standard 5E human traits

**Culture:** Value community and simplicity. Those with wanderlust often seek spirit knowledge or explore ancient ruins.

## ELVES
[[Obojima Tales From The Tall Grass 2024.pdf#page=160|pg 160]] | *Spirit-touched, born to human parents*

**Overview:** Possess deep bond with Spirit Realm. Not a separate people - elves are born to human parents. Uncommon but not rare. Exist with "one foot in two worlds."

**Origin:** Gift from an elder spirit to the people of Okiri. The spirit blessed a human bloodline so their descendants would occasionally birth elves - beings connected to the Spirit Realm.

**Traits:**
- **Size:** Medium (5-6 feet)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft
- **Ethereal Sight:** See creatures in Spirit Realm as glowing outlines (1 hour, Prof Bonus/day)
- **Oaka Mark:** Shimmering birthmark (coin-sized to full limb) grants supernatural abilities and spells

**Lifespan:** Reach adulthood late teens, live less than a century (same as humans).

**Culture:** Often act as intermediaries between spirits and Physical Realm folk. The birth of elves is slowly declining - scholars worry about the day the last elf is born.

## NAKUDAMA
[[Obojima Tales From The Tall Grass 2024.pdf#page=162|pg 162]] | *Amphibious frog-folk, Obojima's first people*

**Overview:** Small amphibious people driven to indulge in all comforts and curiosities. Highly social, love sharing stories. Booming croaking voices fill taverns and tea houses.

**Origin:** Legend says Queen Okumi swam across the cosmic ocean as a tadpole, landed on Mount Arbora, shed her magic tail to lift Obojima above the waves. Oldest civilization on island - ancient architecture notable for water features.

**Traits:**
- **Size:** Small (2-3 feet)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Amphibious:** Breathe air and water
- **Grasping Tongue:** Bonus action, pull object â‰¤5 lbs from 15 ft
- **Latching Tongue:** Bonus action, pull self toward larger creature/surface within 15 ft
- **Standing Leap:** Long jump 20 ft, high jump 15 ft (no running start needed)
- **Languages:** Common, Naku Naku

**Lifespan:** Walk/swim within weeks of birth. Adulthood at 10, average lifespan 45 years.

**Culture:** "Life is too short for anything not worth recounting in an over-the-top tale." Storytelling is cherished tradition - one part experience, two parts showmanship. Prefer cosmopolitan settlements over enclaves.

**Feats:**
- **Nakudama's Electric Bloodline** - lightning resistance, shocking grasp
- **Nakudama's Toxin Bloodline** - poison resistance, toxic touch

## DARA
[[Obojima Tales From The Tall Grass 2024.pdf#page=159|pg 159]] | *Mysterious cyclopean beings, emerged 300 years ago*

**Overview:** Insatiably curious beings who emerged from forests less than 300 years ago. Origin unknown - even dara speculate. Most believe they descend from the enigmatic First Dara. Tend toward observant, wandering lives.

**Types:**
- **Blue Dara:** 6-7 feet tall, blue skin, single large eye, stout, hairless. Gain Knowledge Talisman.
- **Red Dara:** 2-3 feet tall, red skin, two eyes. Gain Might Talisman.

**Lifecycle:** Born from earth through tree roots bearing a "parting glyph." Emerge fully formed but as blank slate. Touch the parting glyph to receive previous dara's memories/identity. Most born in sacred grove at Roa Kala.

When dying, dara capture remaining soul into parting glyph placed on tree - their identity waits for next dara.

**Traits:**
- **Size:** Medium (blue) or Small (red)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Awakened Skills:** Proficiency in 3 skills
- **Create Talisman:** Magic action, create paper talisman (1 hour duration, 1/long rest)
- **Sacred Revelation:** Can see and absorb knowledge from magical glyphs invisible to others

**Lifespan:** Fully functioning within 1 month. Average lifespan ~100 years.

**Culture:** Lead slow-paced observant lives. Gravitate toward wandering. Scrawl notes and sketches constantly. Equally fascinated by bugs and dragons.

## OTHER ANCESTRIES
[[Obojima Tales From The Tall Grass 2024.pdf#page=169|pg 169]]

The source provides context for adapting standard D&D races:
- Tieflings
- Halflings
- Half-Orcs / Orcs
- Gnomes
- Dwarves
- Dragonborn

These are not native to Obojima but can be introduced through player backstory.

---

## FISH FOLK
*Not playable - antagonist faction*

Humanoid fish people dwelling in the Shallows, led by the Lionfish King. Raid coastal settlements, hostile to surface dwellers. The Venomous Rex are a particularly aggressive tribe near Polewater.

---

## SPIRITS
*Not playable - but companion spirits bond with characters*

Elder spirits, companion spirits, and various supernatural beings. Some characters (especially elves) develop deep spirit connections. Witches bond with companion spirits for magic.
