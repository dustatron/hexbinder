# NPCs BY REGION

## Gift of Shuritashi

### Coastal Divers' Lodge (Southern Branch)
- **Paloma Clintock** (female human): Lodge caretaker, fearless diver, launches solo missions vs fish folk.
- **Captain Clintock** (male human): Pointue captain, man of few words, introspective sea explorer.
- **Figby** (male nakudama): Naturalist, musician, chef, freediver, lodge's glue.
- **Leobini** (male human): Old navigator, Captain's friend, rides spirit crab Mr. Tamlin, storyteller.
- **Dahlia** (female elf): Apprentice diver, bookworm, action figure collector, assists with Corruption research.

### Matango Village
- **Reheni** (female human): Current Truffle Prince, won with piglet Gooma, commune with Roghora.
- **Myron** (male human): Lichen Sage druid, ensures magical balance, appeases spirits.
- **Chogo** (male human): Matsutake Gang leader, heavyset, heart of gold, cares for waifs.
- **Marvolio Chanterelle** (male human): Red Chanterelles majordomo, fashionable, uncompromising visionary.
- **Mama Amala** (female human): Reishi Clan head, mid-50s, intense yet gentle, immense spiritual power.
- **Madame Porcini** (female human): Porcini household head, stout, friendly, known for singing, hosts grand parties.
- **Rokoko** (spirit): Large bipedal beetle, Moss Boiler owner/chef, collector of odd ingredients.

### Okiri Village
- **Broad Naldo** (male human): Ox-like build, friendly helper, chatterbox, employs hound Block.
- **Miss Lindley** (female human): Repair specialist, ex-Fish Head Coven Witch, uses transmutation magic.
- **Thim & Torrio Torrelli** (male humans): Adventurous shepherds, joke collectors, formidable slingers.
- **Morna** (female nakudama): Child, spies on strangers, fearless, best-informed in village.
- **Wenneth** (female human): Middle-aged farmer, sword champion, ruthless with blade.
- **Bree** (spirit): Pyramid Melon-like spirit, runs mercantile, specializes in potion ingredients.

### Temple of Shoom
- **Zolde** (female nakudama): Midwives leader, broad build, perpetual scowl, ex-adventurer, can swing sword.
- **Voraro the Parasite** (male nakudama): Ancient Warlock, bloated, water-bound, enthralls nakudama for patron.
- **Beatri** (female nakudama): Missing midwife, last seen clearing second tier.

### Tidewater
- **Vorian** (male elf): Apprentice sand sculptor, humble, shy, true genius behind Great Ferek.
- **Ulmat** (female dara): Tallest in Gift, Pearl's omenspeaker, otherworldly voice, art lover.
- **Gritty Groff** (female human): Sand Witch, lives with giant sand fleas Fleck & Speck, protects pearl.
- **Kenta, Kaz, & Kersh** (humans): Beachcomber kids, live in buried ship, Kersh is Warlock with puffer-fish spirit.
- **Harraga** (nonbinary human): Blind healer acolyte, sweet tooth.
- **Eol** (male human): Burly bald acolyte, frets about cleanliness.
- **Kem** (female nakudama): Loud friendly acolyte, accompanied by black cat spirit Boog.

### Uluwa (Spirit Market)
- **Master of Ceremonies** (spirit): Tall gangly dancing spirit, large eyebrows, never sleeps, sends shadow on errands.
- **Emille the Busker** (male dara): Plays pluckerine, bound to spirit patron, compulsory playing, melancholic.
- **Throth with Eyes Everywhere** (spirit): Collective of floating eyes, market constable, beams light when spots violations.
- **Humble Utzu** (spirit): Tiny with black flame head, smuggler/con artist, refers to self in third person.
- **The Four Orbles** (spirits): Identical small spirits, nimble tumblers, catch magical fish with butterfly nets.
- **Dapo Dapo** (spirits): Fish paste masters duo, both named Dapo, touchy about craft, secretly resent each other.
- **Vymm** (spirit): Spider-like healer, cocoons patients in webbing, serves nefarious clients.
- **Vigor** (spirit): Small rock spirit, assists Vymm.
- **Ferryman** (spirit): Stocky, headless, wears fish necklace, fish heads speak, tosses non-ticket holders.

### Yatamon
- **Cholly** (spirit): Pelican spirit, trolley conductor, scatterbrain, enthusiastic, chats with patrons.
- **Granny Yuzu** (female human): First Age relic collector, lives among yuzu trees, guarded by spirit mastiff Tibor.
- **Gomber** (male human): Hard-boiled detective, straight-shooting, understands partner Beeks perfectly.
- **Beeks** (spirit): Paunchy humanoid bird, only says "Beek!", Gomber's partner, part of Courier Brigade.
- **Tatsu** (male human): Goro Goros gang leader, talks big game, leads magic-tagging vandals.
- **Mikiko** (female human): Goro Goros member, Young Steward, budding Witch, knows pigment potions, feels guilty.
- **Imelda** (female elf): Thief with spirit Choofi, package thief, increasingly audacious, leaves calling card.
- **Choofi** (spirit): Floating fur-covered spirit, discerns objects by smell, helps Imelda steal.
- **Master Hu** (male human): Baker, Happy Joy Cake creator, guru-like, loves making cakes.
- **Postmaster General Miranda Escalante** (female human): Oversees Courier Brigade HQ, trains knights, honest, helpful.
- **Mr. Basingstoke** (male human): Runs Witchery, intimidating Warlock, unaffiliated with covens, has octopus spirit Sorrow.
- **Sorrow** (spirit): Large disturbing octopus, silent, colorless eyes, drifts through walls, emotionless.
- **Dr. Zalia Frond** (female human): AHA archaeologist, affable, excavates undercity, studying First Age.
- **Krocius** (male nakudama): Slender, reserved scholar, secretly searching for Oghmai's prison.
- **Mortimus Fids** (male human): AHA office head curator, self-important snob, makes people grovel.
- **Lula** (spirit): Spirit diner/gambling hall owner, freely travels realms, been in Yatamon for centuries.
- **Bridge Cat** (spirit): Giant sleeping spirit under bridge, eternal slumber, brings longevity/peace, Obojima mystery.
- **Bokka Bokka** (spirit): Jolly chicken spirit, Happy Joy Cake Bakery tour guide, hands out samples.
- **Gojo** (male/species unspecified): Runs Gojo's Ramen Bucket, admires but resents Hakumon, wounded pride.
- **Himitsu** (spirit): Anomalous spirit, runs Arcane Izakaya, alters form, tavern vanishes during day.
- **Jenni** (female human): Witch, general store owner, sells potion ingredients, bosses brothers Hop & Jib, loves adventure stories.
- **Whiskers** (spirit): Caramel spirit cat, lost voice to Witch Meg Wicklowe, needs chattershroom.

## Land of Hot Water

### Chisuay's Teahouse
- **Chisuay** (male human/oni): Tea Master, slender dashing oni sorcerer, charismatic, pompous, struggles with oni nature.
- **Migo** (spirit): Six-armed companion, helps run teahouse, doesn't speak, uses sign language.
- **Indigo** (awakened fossa): Staff majordomo, stuffy, self-important, pushes weight around when Chisuay absent.
- **Fossa Helpers** (awakened fossa): Well-mannered, well-dressed serving staff, resist urge to be nosy.

### Coastal Divers' Lodge (Northern Branch)
- **Holly Clintock** (female human): Younger Clintock daughter, gifted engineer/fabricator, head of lodge, optimistic.
- **Marcel** (male human): Blind lighthouse keeper, broad-shouldered, thick mustache, uses modified fish finder, family to Holly.
- **Disaster** (spirit): Pelican spirit, Marcel's companion, prideful, claims Pelican's Nest named for it.

### Hogstone Hot Springs
- **Adira** (female human): Master healer/herbalist, short, calm, intense presence, runs apothecary.
- **Mocha** (spirit): Adira's companion, enjoys pig guise, provides comfort, takes anthropomorphic pig form when needed.
- **Jollah Everbreeze** (male human): Bard, plays "numinous groove", wind instrument with companion spirit inside.

### Sky Kite Valley
- **Rockwell "Rock" Raley** (male human): Consummate aeronaut, infectious energy, easy charm, simmering ambition.
- **Councilor Jiko** (female human): Oldest of five council leaders, hotheaded, brash, against aeronauts.
- **Olaya** (spirit): Small serpentine spirit, drawn to creativity, transparent, opaque when feeding, asks right questions.
- **Louise** (female human): Master kite maker, unrivaled expertise, ex-partner of Rock, abandoned in Crawling Canopy.
- **Marlon** (male nakudama): Warden of Barges, oversees locks, exceedingly lazy, grumbles nonstop.
- **Rufus & Mal** (male/female human): Brother/sister pub owners, ran Waterline into ground, saved by Rock's boom.
- **Ember** (female/species unspecified): Friendliest kitemonger, offers rentals, teaches novices, closes shop to help.

## Legendary/Historical Figures

- **Shuritashi**: Legendary great spirit, Gift of Shuritashi named for it.
- **Roghora**: Elder pig spirit, fought Great River Spirit Mu, Matango woods domain, grants Truffle Prince communion.
- **Yon**: Great river spirit, takes boar form, associated with Hogstone.
- **Shalwin**: Nakudama sage, led disbanding of monarchy after Oghmai.
- **Rongol**: First caretaker of Pearl, pulled enormous pearl that created Tower of Glass.
- **The Sky King**: Ruler of domain high in clouds, Rock Raley claims to have met.
- **Mr. Hishimoto**: First awakened cat, stone statue in secret glade, honored by vending machine cats.
- **Mu**: Great River Spirit, fought and defeated Roghora.
- **Jumaga the Sky Salamander**: Elder spirit of Mount Arbora.
